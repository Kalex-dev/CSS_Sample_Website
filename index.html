Tokyo Nightlife

Nightime is the right time when in Tokyo! The city really comes alive and has so many interesting places to visit. Wether during the day or at night one of the most amazing aspects about Tokyo is its culinary prowess and diverse selection of delightful treats!

Hotspots

Shinjuku
Akihabara

Akihabara is also known as "Electric Town" and is home to hobbyists known as "Otaku". There are countless retro video game stores and arcades to keep you busy all day!

Shinjuku is home to Kabukicho which is considered an entertainment and red light district. Golden Gai is another popular destination in Shinjuku and is home to about two hundred micro bars all with different themes.
